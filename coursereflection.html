Iteration, Recursion & Backtracking

Iteration

Repeating steps until a condition is met — loops, traversal, pattern generation.

Recursion

Functions calling themselves — used in divide & conquer and tree operations.

Backtracking

Trying all possibilities by reversing previous steps — N-Queens, Sudoku, paths.
