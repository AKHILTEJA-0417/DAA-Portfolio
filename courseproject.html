DAA Course Project

Health & Infrastructure – 10 Detailed Case Studies

1. Multi-Hospital Network Planning for a Tier-2 City

Business Context: A 150 sq km region needs 2 super-specialty, 6 general hospitals, 18 PHCs.

Algorithmic Need: Facility Location / K-Medoids.

Outcome: Saved ₹1,200 crore. 95% reach hospital within 20 minutes.

2. Ambulance Deployment Strategy for Emergency Response

Business Context: 54 ambulances deployed in high-traffic zones.

Algorithmic Need: Dijkstra / A* and Greedy standby-point selection.

Outcome: Response time reduced 16 → 7 minutes; 18% fuel savings.

3. Medical Supply Chain Optimization

Business Context: Distribute drugs, vaccines, dialysis items to 26 facilities.

Algorithmic Need: TSP Approximation + VRP.

Outcome: 32% fuel reduction; stockouts dropped from 21% → 3%.

4. Predictive Dengue Hotspot Identification

Business Context: Use climate + complaint + patient data to predict outbreaks.

Algorithmic Need: DBSCAN + Graph Propagation.

Outcome: Cases reduced 28% via proactive fogging.

5. Budget Allocation for ₹20,000 Crore Health Infrastructure

Business Context: Trauma centers, digital health, PHCs, training.

Algorithmic Need: 0/1 Knapsack + Multi-objective optimization.

Outcome: 21% more coverage; avoided ₹1,800 cr redundant work.

6. Telemedicine Expansion to Rural Areas

Business Context: 7 villages need digital clinics.

Algorithmic Need: MST + Network Flow.

Outcome: Access increased 4×; travel reduced by 63%.

7. Healthcare Workforce Scheduling

Business Context: 730 doctors, 2800 nurses, 1100 technicians.

Algorithmic Need: Hungarian Algorithm + Greedy Heuristics.

Outcome: Staff shortage reduced 17% → 4%.

8. Hospital Patient Flow Optimization

Business Context: 2000 OPD + 600 diagnostics daily = long delays.

Algorithmic Need: Queue Models + Shortest Job First.

Outcome: OPD wait reduced to 55 minutes; lab delay reduced 41%.

9. Smart Health Card & Data Retrieval System

Business Context: Digital card for 32 lakh citizens.

Algorithmic Need: Hashing + AVL Trees + Encryption.

Outcome: Verification time = 0.3 sec; duplicates reduced 26%.

10. Pandemic Preparedness: ICU & Oxygen Planning

Business Context: Need 1800 ICU beds, oxygen plants.

Algorithmic Need: Linear Programming + Dynamic Programming.

Outcome: Region surge-ready in 22 days; oxygen risk ↓ 87%.
