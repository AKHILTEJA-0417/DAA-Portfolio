Course Project: Health & Infrastructure Case Studies

Below are 10 realistic business case studies developed using advanced Design and Analysis of Algorithm techniques applied to a 150 sq km region with a ₹20,000 Crore planning budget.

1. Multi-Hospital Network Planning for a Tier-2 City

Business Context: A metropolitan development authority plans a 3-tier hospital system: 2 super-specialty hospitals, 6 general hospitals, 18 PHCs.

Algorithmic Need: Location Allocation using Facility Location / K-Medoids.

Outcome: Saved ₹1,200 crore in land acquisition. Ensured 95% people reach hospitals within 20 minutes.

2. Ambulance Deployment Strategy for Emergency Response

Business Context: 54 ambulances deployed across traffic-dense areas (IT corridor, market areas, ORR).

Algorithmic Need: Dijkstra / A* for fastest routes, Greedy for selecting 14 standby points.

Outcome: Reduced average response time from 16 → 7 minutes. Annual fuel cost down 18%.

3. Medical Supply Chain Revamp for Government Hospitals

Business Context: Distribution of drugs, vaccines, dialysis consumables to 26 facilities.

Algorithmic Need: TSP Approximation + Vehicle Routing Problem.

Outcome: 32% reduction in fuel usage. Stockout reduced from 21% → 3%.

4. Predictive Hotspot Identification for Dengue Control

Business Context: Prediction using rainfall, complaints, water stagnation, and hospital data.

Algorithmic Need: DBSCAN clustering + Graph propagation modeling.

Outcome: 28% reduction in Dengue cases after proactive fogging.

5. Budget Allocation Plan for ₹20,000 Crore Health Infra

Business Context: Projects: Trauma centers, PHCs, staff training, digital health systems.

Algorithmic Need: 0/1 Knapsack + Multi-objective Optimization.

Outcome: 21% more patient coverage within same budget. Avoided ₹1,800 crore redundant works.

6. Telemedicine Expansion to Rural Fringes

Business Context: 7 villages integrated using 4 digital clinics with hybrid 5G-fiber network.

Algorithmic Need: MST for fiber network + Network Flow for stable video connectivity.

Outcome: Remote consultation access increased 4×. Travel reduced by 63%.

7. Healthcare Workforce Scheduling Across 26 Facilities

Business Context: 730 doctors, 2,800 nurses, 1,100 technicians — shortage during peak days.

Algorithmic Need: Hungarian Algorithm + Greedy Constraint Scheduling.

Outcome: Shortage reduced from 17% → 4%. Saved ₹38 crore per year.

8. Hospital Patient Flow Optimization

Business Context: OPD: 2,000/day, Diagnostics: 600/day, 3+ hour delays.

Algorithmic Need: Queue Models + Shortest-Job-First + internal graph routing.

Outcome: OPD time reduced to 55 mins. Lab time reduced by 41%.

9. Smart Health Card & Data Retrieval System

Business Context: Unified health record for 32 lakh citizens.

Algorithmic Need: Hashing + AVL Trees + Encryption.

Outcome: Auth speed: 0.3 sec. Duplicate records reduced by 26%.

10. Pandemic Preparedness: ICU Bed & Oxygen Planning

Business Context: Planning 1,800 ICU beds, 4 oxygen plants, temporary facilities.

Algorithmic Need: Linear Programming + Dynamic Programming.

Outcome: Surge-ready within 22 days. Oxygen shortage risk ↓ 87%.
